Usability Rules

 - Data Integrity
	A computer shall not cause data harm through action or inaction
	Eg. Deleting repos in github
	Delete is also given at bottom of the settings page so need to scroll down - lesser chance of accidental deletion

 - A computer shud not waste our time or require us to do more work than is strictly necessary
	Never have redundant interactions

 - An interface shud be humane
	Be responsive to human needs and considerate of human frailties

Tesler's Law
	Law of conservation of complexity
	For any system, there is a certain amt of complexity which cant be reduced
