Gestalt's Laws
 - Whole is not same as sum of parts
 - Wholistic view of UI usually from visual perspective
 - Humans have natural compulsion to make order out of disorder

Gestalt's Theory
 - Closure : Mind automatically fills in gaps between elements to perceive complete image
 - Proximity : Mind groups elements that are in same closed region
 - Continuation : Mind groups elements that move in the same direction
 - Symmetry : Humans naturally find symmetry aesthetically pleasing
 - Similarity : Elements that have similar visual appearance are grouped together
 - Figure and Ground (Tree within letters) - Foreground vs Background
